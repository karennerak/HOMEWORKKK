Why should people care about homework?

stress levels

Stress levels are too high for students to handle. Students can not focus on school because of stress. Stress levels increased 20% since 5 years ago

distraction from family

Homework distracts students away from family time. Students have to focus on their work and do their homework instead of having family time.

physical symptoms

It causes headaches, sickness, affects emotion and behavior, phydical health. Can cause high blood pressure, heart disease, obesity, and diabetes.

Solution

Teachers should ease the homework or give online work so students can manage their time and give time for themselves too. Teachers should not give out as much homework and use their TAs to grade the student's work. Students stress about homework a lot.

dare, explore, disrupt

We should disrupt the homework system and change it. Explore apps to help ease stress or explore apps that replace homework
